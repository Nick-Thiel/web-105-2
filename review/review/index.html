Percy Jackson Lightning Thief

A personal favorite book of mine. This book is the first in the Percy Jackson series by Rick Riordan.

Percy Jackson is the main character of the book who ends up losing his mother on the way to a safe haven for demi-god children like himself.

What happens is that they are running from monsters in his mother's car. They end up on the border of the safe haven and Percy's mother ends up getting killed by a Minotaur.
